Freedom Project

This project concerns current and future technologies that could impact art creation.

This project was issued the moment we got into SEP, our teacher made us do this project while we learn to code, basically this project was a year-long project. During the making of this project, I've faced issues such as coming up with inventions and also coding new components such as carousels from bootstrap. I've resolved these problems eventually though, first to resolve my lack of ideas for inventions, I went on google and searched around the art catagory and see what they have right now and come up with ideas that could have big impacts to art creation. Second, to create new components, I needed to learn them, and I did this by going to the official websites of the components creator and looked at their tutorial or official documents.
